Description:
Developed a comprehensive Power BI dashboard to analyze Superstore sales data ($2.26M revenue). I transformed raw data into actionable insights by tracking key performance indicators (KPIs) and identifying critical business trends.

Key Contributions:

- Data Cleaning: Processed and prepared datasets for accurate reporting.

- Trend Analysis: Identified a significant revenue growth trend and seasonal peaks in Q4.

- Performance Metrics: Analyzed category-wise performance, revealing Technology as the leading revenue driver (36.6%).

- Strategic Insights: Segmented top customers and geographic sales distribution to support targeted marketing strategies.
